Frequently Asked Questions

Are these numbers really free to use?

Yes, all our temporary numbers are completely free to use for SMS verification. We support our service through advertisements.

How long do the temporary numbers remain active?

Our numbers typically remain active for 24 hours after first use. This gives you enough time to complete your verification process.

Can I receive multiple SMS on the same number?

Yes, you can receive multiple messages on the same number during its active period. All messages will be displayed in your inbox.

Why isn't my verification code arriving?

Some services may block temporary numbers. If you don't receive your code within 2 minutes, try another number or contact us for assistance.

Is there a limit to how many numbers I can use?

There's no strict limit, but we implement reasonable usage restrictions to prevent abuse and ensure availability for all users.
